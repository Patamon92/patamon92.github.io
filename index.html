OUR STORY

Sharp Productions & Entertainment is the Premiere Aruba wedding & event company. We know that your event starts with you – that’s why we build relationships to personalize your plan so that you and your guests participate in an unforgettable epic celebration that sets the tone for the rest of your life.

Our journey began since 2007, where Sharp Productions & Entertainment has been crafting stories through unforgettable productions and events across the islands. Our commitment extends beyond impressive and magnificent, ensuring that from the tiniest details to the grandest productions, your event is woven into a tale of excellence. At Sharp Productions & Entertainment we embrace the spectrum of possibilities, leaving no stone unturned in creating a narrative that reflects the essence of your unique creative vision.

Our team of diverse, seasoned professionals bring an unparalleled level of talent and personalized service that surpasses your expectations.

Mr. Kock, born into a family of musicians, mastered multiple instruments and stage production from a young age. His expertise led him to collaborate with various production companies on the island. Recognizing the demand for higher quality setups, he established his own entertainment and production company, boasting top-notch gear and talented entertainers.
